Entities

Common Business Structures

Sole Proprietorship

The simplest form of business entity, owned and run by a single individual with no legal distinction between the owner and the business.

Partnership

A business owned by two or more people who share the profits and liabilities. Ideal for professionals teaming up.

Private Limited Company

A separate legal entity offering limited liability protection to its shareholders, making it the most preferred structure for businesses.
